HD 21749b planet

Nasa's Transiting Exoplanet Survey Satellite (TESS) discovered new planet this planet has been named "HD 21749b" It orbits around a bright dwarf star about 53 light years away from the earth, in the constellation 'Recticulum'. It is the third planet discovered by TESS. The other two planets discovered by TESS are 'Pi Mensae b' and 'LHS 3844b'. TESS was launched in April 2018.
