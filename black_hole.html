The Super massive black hole

It is the center photo of our milky way galaxy of star "Sagittarius A*" , which has been captured by our "THE EVENT HORIZONS TELESCOPE" , now you all might be thinking that the black hole doesn't emit light so how scientist had taken this photo , why this photo is so special and what does this discovery means for humans.


Answer for the first question is that the yellow and the orange color which you are able to see in the above photo is the hot plasma revolving around it. It is so special because it is only the second photo taken of a black hole and the credits for this image goes to the same team which has taken the picture of the first black hole "The Messier 87 Black Hole" in the year 2019 .
